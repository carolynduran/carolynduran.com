News Updates

Fall 2017

 

Carolyn will be attending the University of Michigan where she plans to compete for the Club Gymnastics Team.

Summer 2017

 

During the summer, Carolyn spent much of her time at the gym continuing training and coaching developmental classes. She was also a gymnastics summer camp counselor at both the YMCA and Woodward Camp PA. At Woodward, she had an amazing time meeting Olympians, learning new coaching techniques and spotting, and watching the kids learn new skills every week!

June 29-July 1, 2016

 

At YMCA National Gymnastics Championships in Savannah, GA, Carolyn competed in two days of competition. She placed 2nd in the All-Around and earned All-American status for the third consecutive year. She was an individual event finalist on all four apparatus, earning 2nd on vault, 2nd on bars, 1st on beam, and 1st on floor. She earned her level 9/10 career high all-around score of 37.0, and her team placed 2nd.

April 30, 2017

 

Carolyn traveled to Level 9 Eastern Nationals in Lansing, MI to compete for Region VII. She placed 2nd on bars and her team won 1st place.

April 8, 2017

 

Carolyn traveled to Region VII Championships in Landover, MD. She placed 5th on beam and 7th in the all-around, which qualified her to Eastern Nationals.

March 18, 2017

 

Carolyn traveled to PA State Championships in Lock Haven, PA. She placed 3rd on vault, 2nd on bars, and 2nd on floor. She was the PA State Champion in the All-Around.

Winter 2016 - 2017

 

In addition to competing for Phoenixville Gymnastics, Carolyn competed for her high school's dive team. Highlights from her season include winning multiple dual meets and placing 8th at the Perkiomen Valley Invitational. She qualified for District Championships for the second consecutive year.

December 2016 - March 2017

 

Carolyn had a great start to her senior year. Highlights from her regular season competitions include competing level 10 at the Liberty Cup and placing 2nd on floor and 3rd in the All-Around, placing 2nd All-Around at the Parkettes Invitational, placing 1st on bars and 1st in the All-Around at the Pink Invitational, and placing 1st on vault and 1st All-Around at YMCA League Championships.

June 29 - July 3, 2016

 

At YMCA National Gymnastics Championships in Long Beach, CA, Carolyn competed in three days of competition. She placed 7th in the All-Around and earned All-American status for the second consecutive year. During the competition, she also earned her highest scores on the vault (9.55), beam(9.2), and floor (9.45). She was an individual event finalist on those three events, earning 2nd on vault, 5th on beam, and 3rd on floor.

 

April 9, 2016

 

Carolyn competed at USAG Region 7 Championships in Hampton, VA. She placed 7th on uneven bars. Her next meet will be YMCA Nationals in Long Beach, CA in June.

 

 

March 6, 2016

 

Carolyn competed at the YMCA Eastern PA Girls Gymnastics League Championships in Hatboro, PA. She scored multiple season-highs, including 2nd place on beam and 1st place on bars. She also earned a new high All-Around score, placing 1st with a 36.425.

February 28, 2016

 

Carolyn's third USAG meet of the season was the Pink Invitational in Philadelphia, PA, hosted by AJS Pancott Gymnastics. There, she scored her highest All-Around score this season, placing 1st in her age group. She also earned 4th place on beam, 3rd place on vault, 1st place on floor and 1st place on bars.

February 6, 2016

 

Carolyn competed at the

Dave Moskovitz

Invitational at Ursinus College, hosted by Berks East Gymnastics. She placed 2nd on bars and 2nd in the All-Around.

December 13, 2015

 

Carolyn competed at a YMCA meet at her home gym, Phoenixville Gymnastics. There she placed 4th on vault, 1st on bars, 1st on beam, and 2nd on floor. She earned 1st place in the All-Around.

December 4, 2015

 

Carolyn's first USAG meet of the season was the Liberty Cup in Oaks, PA , hosted by Aerials Gymnastics. She placed 2nd on vault, 4th on bars, 2nd on beam, 5th on floor, and 2nd in the All-Around.

June 24-27, 2015

 

Carolyn participated in the 2015 YMCA National Championship in Wichita, Kansas. The competition consisted of two days of All-Around competition, with a third day for top-placing finalists on each apparatus. Carolyn placed top 8 on vault, bars, and floor. After the final competition, she placed 4th on vault, 6th on floor, and 4th All-Around. She was NATIONAL UNEVEN BAR CHAMPION!!! Throughout the competition she earned her highest All-Around score of the season and her team placed 3rd in the team competition.

 

April 26, 2015

 

Carolyn's eleventh meet was the Pennsylvania State YMCA Championships at YMCA of the Brandywine Valley. She placed 2nd on vault, 1st on bars, 5th on beam, and 3rd on floor. She placed 2nd in the All-Around and her team placed 1st in the team competition. Carolyn's next competition will be YMCA National Championships in Wichita, Kansas in June.

April 19, 2015

 

Carolyn's tenth meet was the USAG Level 9 Region VII Championships at Ursinus College. She gained valuable experience at the Regional level and improved on her recent bars and beam results. She placed 3rd on vault in the region.

April 12, 2015

 

Carolyn's ninth meet was the Eastern Pennsylvania YMCA Girls' Gymnastics League Championships, hosted by Phoenixville.  She placed 2nd on vault, 2nd on bars, 1st on beam, 4th on floor, and 1st in the all-around. Her team placed 1st in the all-around competition!

March 28, 2015

 

Carolyn's eighth meet of the season was USAG Level 9 States in Williamsport, Pennsylvania. She placed 5th on vault and qualified for Region VII Level 9 Championships.

March 8, 2015

 

Carolyn's seventh meet was a YMCA meet at the Upper Bucks YMCA.  She placed 2nd on vault, 1st on bars, 3rd on beam, and 1st in the all-around. Her next meet is at USAG States in Williamsport, PA.

 

February 28, 2015

 

Carolyn's sixth meet of the season was the Pink Invitational hosted by AJS Pancott NTC which raises money and awareness for women and families affected by breast cancer. She attained her highest scores of the season on vault and floor. She placed 2nd on vault, 3rd on bars, 7th on beam, 2nd on floor, and 3rd in the all-around.

February 8, 2015

 

Carolyn's fifth meet of the season was an Invitational at the Hatboro YMCA, where she  improved considerably on the uneven bars.  She placed 1st on the uneven bars, and 3rd in the all-around competition.

January 18, 2015

 

Carolyn's fourth meet of the season was an Invitational at the Upper Bucks YMCA, where she achieved her highest all-around score of the season so far!  She placed 1st on the vault, 1st on the uneven bars, 2nd on beam, 1st on floor and 1st in the all-around competition.

January 10, 2015

 

Carolyn's third meet of the season was a USAG meet at the Hanover YMCA. She placed 2nd on the vault, 2nd on the uneven bars, 2nd on beam, 2nd on floor and 2nd in the all-around competition.

December 14, 2014

 

Carolyn's second meet of the season was a YMCA League meet at the Hatboro YMCA. She placed 1st on the uneven bars, 1st on vault, 2nd on floor,  and 4th in the all-around competition.

December 5, 2014

 

Carolyn competed in her first meet of the 2014-2015 season at the Liberty Cup, hosted by Aerials Gymnastics. She placed 2nd on the uneven bars and 4th in the all-around competition. Her level 9 team placed 1st.
